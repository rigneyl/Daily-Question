# ✅ **Daily Question — App Overview (GitHub-Ready)**

## **What This App Is**

Daily Question is a minimalist reflection app where **everyone gets one new question per day** — deep, fun, or thought-provoking.
You answer first, *then* you unlock everyone else’s answers.
It’s social, but without feeds, scrolling, or noise.

---

## **Core Features**

* One question released daily
* Clean, focused “Today’s Question” screen
* Write your answer and submit
* View community answers after submitting
* Local streak tracker
* History of past answers
* No social feed, no infinite scroll

---

## **Screens**

### **Today’s Question**

* Displays the daily question
* Buttons: *Answer Now*, *See Responses*
* Shows response count

### **Answer Screen**

* Text area for your response
* Submit button
* Shows avatars + response count

### **Responses Screen**

* List of all responses for the day
* Badges like “You”, “Popular”, “Most liked”
* Buttons: *View History*, *Invite Friends*

### **Completion Screen**

* Shows confirmation badge
* Button: *See Today’s Answers*

### **History Screen**

* List of your past answers
* Simple preview and date
* Tap to view past entries
